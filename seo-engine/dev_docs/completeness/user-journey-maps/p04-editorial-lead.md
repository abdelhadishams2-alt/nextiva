# P04: Editorial Lead (James) — User Journey Map

**Persona:** Editorial Lead at a news/media organization
**Tier:** Enterprise ($8-12K/mo)
**Priorities:** Voice intelligence (10/10), quality assurance (10/10), brand consistency
**Deal-breakers:** Voice/brand consistency, high-quality output
**Daily workflow:** Review voice match scores, check quality reports, approve/reject articles, manage writer personas

---

## Phase 1: First-Time Setup

**Goal:** Establish brand voice standards in the platform, configure quality gates, set editorial approval workflows.

### Touchpoints
| Step | Screen | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Login/Signup (`/signup`) | Sign up via enterprise SSO; role assigned as "Editorial Lead" by admin |
| 2 | Onboarding Wizard (`/onboarding`) | Select "Editorial/Brand" path; upload style guide PDF; specify voice attributes |
| 3 | Voice Profiles (`/voice`) | Create primary brand voice profile; define tone dimensions (formal/casual, authoritative/friendly) |
| 4 | Blueprint Gallery (`/blueprints`) | Browse article blueprints; select templates matching editorial standards |
| 5 | Quality Report (`/articles/[id]/quality`) | Review quality signal definitions; set organization-wide minimum thresholds |

### Emotions
- **Protective** — "Our brand voice took years to develop. This platform better not butcher it."
- **Skeptical** — "AI cannot replicate the nuance of our editorial voice."
- **Intrigued** when voice profile configuration offers granular controls.

### Pain Points
- Style guide PDF upload needs to actually parse and extract voice attributes — not just store the file.
- Voice profile setup is abstract; hard to know if configuration is "right" without seeing output.
- Quality thresholds set too high may reject everything; too low may let poor content through.

### Opportunities
- Parse uploaded style guide to auto-suggest voice profile settings (tone, vocabulary, sentence structure).
- Generate a sample article immediately after voice profile creation so James can evaluate.
- Offer a "calibration mode" — show 5 sample articles and let James rate each, refining the voice model.

---

## Phase 2: Learning (Week 1)

**Goal:** Generate first articles, evaluate voice fidelity, establish the review-approve-reject loop.

### Touchpoints
| Step | Screen | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Article Pipeline (`/articles`) | Generate 5 test articles across different content types |
| 2 | Article Detail (`/articles/[id]`) | Read each article carefully; check for voice consistency, factual claims, tone |
| 3 | Quality Report (`/articles/[id]/quality`) | Deep-dive into voice match signal; compare across the 5 articles |
| 4 | Voice Profiles (`/voice`) | Adjust voice profile parameters based on first-generation feedback |
| 5 | Article Pipeline (`/articles`) | Reject 3 articles, approve 2; add editorial notes to rejections |

### Emotions
- **Critical** — line-editing every sentence, looking for "AI voice" tells.
- **Frustrated** when voice match score says 9/10 but James rates it 6/10 — calibration mismatch.
- **Encouraged** when one article genuinely sounds like their brand.

### Pain Points
- Voice match score may not align with James's subjective assessment — needs human feedback loop.
- Rejection workflow lacks inline annotation — James wants to highlight specific sentences and explain why.
- No A/B comparison between two voice profiles generating the same article.

### Opportunities
- Add "Rate This Voice Match" feedback mechanism that trains the voice model over time.
- Build inline editorial annotation on Article Detail — highlight text, add comments, suggest rewrites.
- Offer A/B voice comparison mode — generate same article with 2 profiles, display side-by-side.

## Phase 3: Competency (Weeks 2-4)

**Goal:** Scale editorial review, onboard writers to voice profiles, establish style consistency across team.

### Touchpoints
| Step | Screen | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Voice Profiles (`/voice`) | Create additional voice profiles for sub-brands, columnists, and content verticals |
| 2 | User Management (`/admin/users`) | Assign writers to specific voice profiles; set per-writer permissions |
| 3 | Article Pipeline (`/articles`) | Review articles generated by team members; enforce voice consistency |
| 4 | Quality Report (`/articles/[id]/quality`) | Track voice match trends over time — is the model improving? |
| 5 | Blueprint Gallery (`/blueprints`) | Create editorial-approved blueprints with embedded voice requirements |

### Emotions
- **Authoritative** — "I am building the brand voice system for the entire newsroom."
- **Relieved** when quality trends show consistent improvement.
- **Overwhelmed** if review queue grows faster than James can process.

### Pain Points
- No way to see aggregate voice consistency scores across all recent articles.
- Reviewing every article manually does not scale; needs automated first-pass filtering.
- Multiple voice profiles can create confusion — which profile applies to which content type?

### Opportunities
- Build a "Voice Consistency Dashboard" showing aggregate scores, trend lines, and outliers.
- Auto-reject articles below voice threshold; only surface borderline cases for human review.
- Add voice profile assignment rules — auto-select profile based on content type, category, or target publication.

## Phase 4: Mastery (Month 2+)

**Goal:** Codify editorial standards into automated workflows, measure brand consistency at scale, train junior editors.

### Touchpoints
| Step | Screen | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Voice Profiles (`/voice`) | Refine voice models using accumulated feedback data; export voice guidelines |
| 2 | Performance (`/performance`) | Correlate voice consistency scores with content performance metrics |
| 3 | Plugin Configuration (`/settings`) | Configure automated editorial workflows (auto-reject < 7/10 voice, auto-approve > 9/10) |
| 4 | Dashboard Home (`/`) | Customize dashboard to show editorial KPIs (voice scores, rejection rate, time-to-approve) |
| 5 | Blueprint Gallery (`/blueprints`) | Version-control editorial blueprints; maintain a style evolution log |

### Emotions
- **Strategic** — "Voice data proves that brand consistency drives engagement."
- **Proud** presenting voice analytics to executive leadership.
- **Concerned** about voice model drift over time without continuous calibration.

### Pain Points
- Voice model may drift as article corpus grows — needs periodic recalibration reminders.
- Correlating voice scores with performance requires combining data from multiple screens.
- No way to export voice guidelines in a format shareable with freelance writers outside the platform.

### Opportunities
- Build automatic voice model drift detection with monthly recalibration prompts.
- Create a unified "Editorial Analytics" view combining voice, quality, and performance data.
- Offer voice guideline export as a styled PDF or shareable link for external collaborators.

## Phase 5: Error Recovery

### Voice Profile Corruption
- **Trigger:** Voice profile settings accidentally overwritten by a team member.
- **Expected behavior:** Version history on voice profiles; one-click restore to previous version.
- **Pain point:** Corrupted voice profile affects all subsequent article generation before it is noticed.
- **Solution:** Immutable voice profile versioning; changes require approval from profile owner. Show "This profile was modified 2 hours ago by [user]" notification.

### Quality Score Disagreement
- **Trigger:** James consistently disagrees with automated quality scores.
- **Expected behavior:** Feedback mechanism that adjusts scoring model to align with editorial judgment.
- **Pain point:** If James cannot trust scores, the entire quality gate system breaks down.
- **Solution:** "Disagree with score" button that logs James's actual rating; after 20+ disagreements, offer to recalibrate the model for this organization.

### Accidental Article Approval
- **Trigger:** Approved an article that should have been rejected; article moves to publish queue.
- **Expected behavior:** "Revoke Approval" action that pulls article back from Publish Manager to Review stage.
- **Pain point:** If article was already published, damage to brand voice is live.
- **Solution:** Two-stage approval for enterprise: "Approve for Publish Queue" then "Approve for Publishing." Revoke at either stage. Grace period between queue and publish.

### Session Timeout During Review
- **Trigger:** James spends 30+ minutes reading and annotating an article; session expires.
- **Expected behavior:** All annotations and draft comments auto-saved; restored on re-login.
- **Pain point:** Losing 30 minutes of detailed editorial feedback is a deal-breaker.
- **Solution:** Auto-save editorial annotations every 30 seconds to server. Session refresh happens silently. If hard expiry, restore full state including scroll position.

## Empty State Strategy

### Voice Profiles (`/voice`)
- Show a guided voice profile creation wizard with 3 steps: (1) Upload style guide or describe voice in free text, (2) Adjust tone sliders, (3) Generate and rate a sample article.
- Message: "Your brand voice is the foundation of content quality. Let us capture it in 5 minutes."
- CTA: "Create Your First Voice Profile"

### Article Pipeline (`/articles`)
- Show empty pipeline columns with a single prompt card: "Generate a test article to see ChainIQ's voice matching in action."
- Include a "Quick Start" option that generates one article using a trending topic + the newly created voice profile.
- Message: "Articles generated here are scored for voice consistency before they reach your review queue."

### Quality Report (`/articles/[id]/quality`)
- Not applicable as an empty state (requires an article to exist). Instead, link from the empty Article Pipeline to a "What Quality Scoring Measures" explainer page with definitions of all 7 signals.

## "What's New" Preferences

This persona values:
- Voice intelligence improvements (new voice attributes, better matching algorithms)
- Quality scoring methodology changes (new signals, recalibrated weights)
- Editorial workflow features (inline annotation, approval chains, review delegation)
- Brand consistency analytics and reporting
- Integration with editorial tools (Google Docs, Notion, content style linting)

**Preferred delivery:** In-app notification on Voice Profiles and Quality Report screens when relevant features ship. No email digest — James lives in the platform daily and prefers contextual discovery. "What's Changed in Voice Scoring" modal on first visit after algorithm update.
