## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy the template** into a new file: `dev_docs/reviews/week-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
2. **Fill it out honestly** -- this is for you, not for a manager. If you shipped nothing, say so and explain why.
3. **Review last week's review** before filling out this week's. Did you follow through on your "one thing to change"?
4. **Update STATUS.md** if the review surfaces anything that should be tracked.
5. **Time-box to 30 minutes.** If you can't fill it out in 30 minutes, the review is too detailed.

### Why This Matters for Solo Developers

Without a team to provide feedback, it is easy to drift. Common failure modes:
- **Scope creep without noticing** -- velocity drops but you feel busy
- **Quality erosion** -- skipping tests "just this once" becomes a habit
- **Blocked without escalation** -- a blocker sits for days because no one asked about it
- **No celebration** -- shipping features without acknowledging progress leads to burnout

This review catches all four. It takes 30 minutes per week and saves hours of debugging, rework, and lost context.
