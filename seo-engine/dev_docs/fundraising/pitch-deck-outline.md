---

## 1. Deck Philosophy

This pitch deck follows the "problem-solution-traction" arc that MENA tech investors (STV, Wamda Capital, 500 Global MENA) respond to best. The narrative is simple: a massive market (Arabic digital content) has zero AI tooling. ChainIQ fills that gap with a working product and early traction. Every slide earns the right to show the next slide — if the investor isn't curious after slide 3, the deck has failed.

Key principles for this deck:
- Lead with the market gap, not the technology. Investors fund markets, not features.
- Show the product, don't describe it. Slide 5 should be a live demo screenshot or video embed, not bullet points about agents.
- Be honest about being a solo founder. Frame it as capital efficiency, not a weakness.
- Use Arabic text where relevant — it demonstrates the product and signals market authenticity.
- Keep each slide to one idea. If it takes more than 6 seconds to grasp the slide's point, it has too much content.

### Slide 2: Problem

- Point 4: Result: Arabic publishers either can't scale content, or scale with declining quality.

**Speaking notes:** "There are over 420 million Arabic speakers and a digital content market growing at [X]% annually. Yet every AI content tool on the market was built for English. Jasper, Surfer, Clearscope — they don't understand Arabic morphology, they can't handle dialects, and they produce content that native Arabic readers immediately identify as machine-generated. ## 4. Deck Delivery Guidelines

**For in-person/Zoom presentations:**
- Present slides 1-12 in 10-12 minutes
- Leave 8-10 minutes for Q&A
- Have appendix slides ready for technical questions
- Demo the product live if internet is reliable; use recorded Loom backup otherwise

**For email/cold send:**
- Convert to PDF (no editable deck)
- Include 1-page executive summary as cover letter
- Follow up within 48 hours if no response
- Track opens with DocSend or Pitch

**For warm introductions:**
- Send deck 24 hours before the meeting
- Prepare 3 questions about the investor's portfolio (show you've done homework)
- Reference their MENA investments specifically
