## LinkedIn Strategy (PRIMARY)

### Why LinkedIn
- MENA business community is highly active on LinkedIn
- Agency and publisher decision-makers spend time here
- Long-form content performs well — aligns with thought leadership
- Arabic content is underrepresented, creating opportunity
- Direct access to procurement / marketing heads at target publishers

### Posting Schedule

| Day | Post Type | Pillar | Notes |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| Tuesday | Educational / Thought leadership | Educational (40%) | Tips, trends, insights |
| Wednesday | Product or Social proof | Product (30%) / Social proof (20%) | Alternating weeks |
| Friday | Culture / Engagement | Culture (10%) or Poll | Lighter tone, engagement-focused |

### Content Formats

#### 1. Text Posts (50% of content)
- Hook in first 2 lines (the "see more" fold)
- 150-300 words
- Line breaks for readability
- End with question or CTA
- Example hook: "Less than 2% of AI content tools support Arabic natively. Here's why that's a $4B problem..."

#### 2. Carousel Posts (20% of content)
- 6-10 slides
- Bold headline per slide, minimal body text
- Brand colors: dark navy + gold accents
- Topics: "7 Agents Explained", "Arabic AI Content Checklist", comparison charts
- Created in Canva, exported as PDF

#### 3. Native Video (15% of content)
- 60-90 seconds max
- Captions always (many watch muted)
- Vertical or square format
- Product demos, quick tips, testimonial clips
- Upload natively (not YouTube links — LinkedIn suppresses external links)

#### 4. Polls (10% of content)
- 2-4 options
- Industry-relevant questions
- Use to gather market intelligence
- Examples: "What's your biggest Arabic content challenge?" / "How many AI tools does your team use?"

#### 5. Document/PDF Posts (5% of content)
- Gated reports, checklists, frameworks
- "Comment 'GUIDE' and I'll DM you the PDF" mechanic for lead generation

### LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** "Building ChainIQ — AI Content Intelligence for Arabic Publishers | Founder @ Chain Reaction SEO"
- **Banner:** ChainIQ brand visual with tagline + URL
- **About:** 3-paragraph story: problem, solution, invitation to connect
- **Featured:** Pin top-performing posts, product demo video, case study
- **Activity:** Engage with 10 relevant posts/day (comment, not just like)

### LinkedIn Company Page
- **Name:** ChainIQ
- **Tagline:** "AI Content Intelligence for Arabic Publishers"
- **Description:** Full value proposition, tier overview, CTA to website
- **Content:** Mirror personal posts 1 day later (personal posts get more reach)
- **Showcase page:** Consider for Arabic-language content specifically

### Engagement Strategy
- Respond to every comment within 4 hours
- Comment on 10 relevant posts/day from target audience
- Join and participate in: MENA Marketing, Arabic Content Creators, SaaS Growth groups
- DM strategy: connect with every new follower, personalized message (not sales pitch)
- Tag relevant people in posts (with permission) for amplification

## Twitter/X Strategy (SECONDARY)

### Why Twitter/X
- SEO and tech community is active here
- Quick takes and threads work for technical content
- AI/ML community follows product builders
- Lower effort than LinkedIn — good for reach extension

### Posting Schedule
- **Tuesday:** Thread or technical insight (aligns with LinkedIn educational post)
- **Thursday:** Quick take, link to blog post, or product update

### Content Formats

#### 1. Threads (1/week)
- 5-8 tweets
- Number each tweet (1/7, 2/7...)
- Hook tweet must stand alone
- End with CTA (follow, visit, reply)
- Topics: "How we built X", "Why Arabic AI is hard", "SEO tips for publishers"

#### 2. Quick Takes (1/week)
- Single tweet or 2-tweet combo
- Hot takes on AI content industry
- Response to trending SEO/AI news
- Product milestone announcements

### Twitter Profile
- **Bio:** "Building @ChainIQ — AI Content Intelligence for Arabic Publishers. Founder @ChainReactionSEO. Arabic-first AI."
- **Pinned tweet:** Product announcement or best-performing thread
- **Header:** Same as LinkedIn banner for brand consistency

### Hashtag Strategy (Twitter)

**English hashtags:**
- Always: #ContentIntelligence #ArabicAI #ChainIQ
- Rotate: #SEO #ContentMarketing #AIContent #SaaS #MENA #PublishingTech
- Trending (use when relevant): #AI #MachineLearning #NLP

**Arabic hashtags:**
- Always: #ذكاء_المحتوى #الذكاء_الاصطناعي_العربي
- Rotate: #تسويق_المحتوى #سيو #الناشرون_العرب #تقنية
- Market-specific: #السعودية #الإمارات #مصر

**Hashtag rules:**
- LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags at end of post
- Twitter: 2-3 hashtags inline or at end
- Never more than 5 on any platform
- Track performance monthly, retire underperformers

## YouTube Strategy (TERTIARY)

### Why YouTube
- Product demos need visual medium
- SEO value — YouTube is the second-largest search engine
- Tutorial content has long shelf life
- Builds trust through seeing the product in action

### Content Series

#### 1. Product Demos (monthly)
- 2-3 minutes
- Screen recording with voiceover
- Show real output, real dashboard
- CTA: free trial link in description
- Titles: "ChainIQ Demo: [Feature] in Action"

#### 2. Tutorial Series (bi-weekly when active)
- 5-7 minutes each
- Step-by-step walkthrough
- Series: "Getting Started with ChainIQ"
  - Episode 1: "Generate Your First Article in 5 Minutes"
  - Episode 2: "Connecting Google Search Console for Content Intelligence"
  - Episode 3: "Using the Quality Gate to Ensure Content Excellence"
  - Episode 4: "Understanding Your Content Performance Dashboard"
  - Episode 5: "Advanced: Custom Agent Configuration"

#### 3. Customer Testimonials (quarterly)
- 3-5 minutes
- Interview format (Zoom recording, cleaned up)
- Focus on results and ROI
- Arabic subtitles for English videos, English subtitles for Arabic

#### 4. "Behind the Algorithm" Series (monthly)
- 5-8 minutes
- How the 7-agent pipeline works technically
- Whiteboard-style or slide-based
- Episodes: Research Agent, Writing Agent, Quality Gate, SEO Agent, etc.
- Targets technical audience and builds credibility

### YouTube SEO
- Keyword-optimized titles (research via TubeBuddy / vidIQ free tier)
- Custom thumbnails: consistent brand template, face + text overlay
- Description: first 2 lines are the hook (visible without expanding), then full description, timestamps, links
- Tags: 10-15 relevant tags per video
- Playlists: organize by series
- End screens: subscribe CTA + next video suggestion
- Cards: link to relevant product pages

### Production Specs (Bootstrap Phase)
| Element | Tool | Cost |
|---------|------|------|
| Screen recording | OBS Studio | Free |
| Voiceover | Built-in mic / Blue Yeti | $0-$50 |
| Editing | DaVinci Resolve | Free |
| Thumbnails | Canva Pro | $13/mo (shared) |
| Subtitles | YouTube auto + manual correction | Free |

### Production Upgrade Path (When Revenue Allows)
- Phase 1 ($0): Screen recordings with voiceover, Canva thumbnails
- Phase 2 ($200/mo): Freelance video editor, better mic, intro/outro animations
- Phase 3 ($500/mo): Professional videographer for testimonials, motion graphics

## Crisis & Reputation Management

### Monitoring
- Google Alerts for "ChainIQ", "Chain Reaction SEO"
- LinkedIn notifications (respond within 4 hours)
- Twitter mentions (respond within 2 hours)
- Monthly brand sentiment review

### Response Guidelines
- **Positive feedback:** Thank publicly, amplify
- **Feature requests:** Acknowledge, log in roadmap, follow up when shipped
- **Bugs / complaints:** Acknowledge publicly, move to DM for resolution, follow up publicly when resolved
- **Competitive attacks:** Do not engage directly; respond with positive product messaging
- **Misinformation:** Correct factually and politely with evidence

## Monthly Review Process

### First Monday of each month:
1. Pull analytics from all platforms
2. Identify top 3 performing posts — what made them work?
3. Identify bottom 3 — why did they underperform?
4. Review follower growth vs. targets
5. Adjust content mix based on engagement data
6. Plan next month's content themes
7. Update hashtag strategy based on reach data
