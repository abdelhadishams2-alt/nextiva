## Content Production Workflow

### Weekly Rhythm
1. **Friday (prior week):** Outline next week's blog posts and LinkedIn copy
2. **Saturday/Sunday:** Draft blog posts, create visuals
3. **Monday:** Publish blog post #1, schedule LinkedIn posts for the week
4. **Thursday:** Publish blog post #2 (often Arabic mirror or new topic)
5. **Daily:** Engage with comments and DMs within 4 hours

### Tools & Stack
| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| WordPress | Blog hosting (existing) | $0 (already have) |

### Content Repurposing Matrix
- **1 blog post** becomes:
  - 1 LinkedIn post (key takeaway)
  - 1 Arabic mirror/summary post
  - 1 Twitter/X thread (optional)

## Editorial Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional** but not corporate — approachable expert
- **Arabic-first** positioning — always mention Arabic capability prominently
- **Data-driven** — include stats, percentages, benchmarks wherever possible
- **Builder authenticity** — solo dev building something meaningful for an underserved market

### SEO Standards
- Every blog post targets 1 primary keyword + 2-3 secondary keywords
- Meta title under 60 characters, meta description under 155 characters
- Internal linking: every post links to at least 2 other ChainIQ posts
- Schema markup: Article, FAQ, HowTo where applicable
- Image alt text in both English and Arabic

### Arabic Content Standards
- Native Arabic writing — not machine-translated English
- Right-to-left (RTL) formatting verified before publish
- Arabic keyword research via Google Keyword Planner (SA, UAE, EG markets)
- Diacritics (tashkeel) used where ambiguity exists
