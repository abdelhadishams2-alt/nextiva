# ChainIQ Master Marketing Strategy — 90-Day Launch Plan

**Document:** Step 21 — Marketing Strategy & Channel Plan
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-28

---

## Executive Summary

This 90-day plan takes ChainIQ from pre-launch stealth to active market presence in the MENA publisher ecosystem. The strategy is designed for a solo developer with bootstrap budget constraints — every activity is prioritized by effort-to-impact ratio. The plan focuses on organic credibility-building in Month 1, coordinated launch in Month 2, and paid amplification in Month 3.

**Total 90-Day Budget:** $2,400-3,600
**Primary Market:** MENA Arabic publishers and content agencies
**Secondary Market:** Global agencies serving Arabic-speaking audiences
**Success Metric:** 5 paying customers by Day 90

|---|---|---|---|

**Email Sequence (per prospect):**

**Target Response Rate:** 15-20% open rate, 5-8% reply rate, 3-5 demo bookings from 50 outreach emails.

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| SRMG case study not ready for Month 2 | Medium | High | Prepare synthetic "Platform Capabilities" showcase as backup |
| Product Hunt launch underperforms | Medium | Low | PH is one channel — not dependent on it for pipeline |
| Low email response rates | Medium | Medium | A/B test subject lines, increase volume, shift to warm introductions |
| LinkedIn ads don't convert | Medium | Low | $200 test budget limits downside; pivot to content syndication |
| Solo dev capacity constraints | High | High | Prioritize ruthlessly — sales-generating activities first, brand-building second |
| Arabic market slower to adopt SaaS | Medium | High | Lead with free trial, extend pilot programs, focus on relationship-building |

## Post-90-Day Horizon

After the initial 90 days, the marketing strategy shifts based on results:

- **If 5+ paying customers:** Double down on what's working. Increase LinkedIn ad budget to $500/mo. Hire first part-time marketer.
- **If 2-4 paying customers:** Extend pilot programs. Increase demo volume. Refine messaging based on objection patterns.
- **If 0-1 paying customers:** Conduct customer development interviews. Reassess pricing. Consider pivoting Growth tier to freemium model.
