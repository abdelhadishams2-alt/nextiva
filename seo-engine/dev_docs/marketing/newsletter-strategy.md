## Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Trial Users
- **Definition:** Signed up for free trial, active or recently expired
- **Content emphasis:** Product tips, getting-started guides, upgrade nudge
- **Unique section:** "Trial tip of the month" — one actionable thing to try
- **CTA focus:** Activate features / upgrade to paid

### Segment 2: Paying Customers
- **Definition:** Active subscribers on any paid plan
- **Content emphasis:** Advanced features, roadmap previews, community
- **Unique section:** "Power user tip" — advanced workflow or configuration
- **CTA focus:** Expand usage, refer colleagues, upgrade tier

### Segment 3: Enterprise Prospects
- **Definition:** Leads from enterprise landing page, demo requests, or tagged as enterprise
- **Content emphasis:** Case studies, ROI data, enterprise features, security/compliance
- **Unique section:** "Enterprise spotlight" — relevant case study or feature
- **CTA focus:** Book a demo / start enterprise trial

### Segment 4: Inactive / Leads
- **Definition:** Subscribed via lead magnet or waitlist but no trial/purchase
- **Content emphasis:** Educational content, market trends, soft product mentions
- **Unique section:** None (standard template)
- **CTA focus:** Start free trial

## Template Structure

### Header
- ChainIQ logo
- "The ChainIQ Pulse — [Month Year]"
- One-line hook: what's inside this issue

### Section 1: Hero Feature (200-300 words)
- The most important thing this month
- Could be: major product launch, industry trend, key insight
- Includes 1 image/screenshot
- Format: narrative opening, key points, link to full article
- Example: "Arabic Content Marketing Just Changed: Google's New MENA Quality Signals"

### Section 2: 3 Tips of the Month (150-200 words total)
- Quick, actionable tips related to Arabic content, SEO, or AI
- Numbered list format
- Each tip: 1-2 sentences + link to deeper content
- Mix: 1 product tip, 1 SEO tip, 1 industry insight
- Example:
  1. "Use ChainIQ's dialect selector to target Gulf Arabic — engagement rates are 34% higher than MSA for Saudi audiences"
  2. "Google's helpful content update now weighs E-E-A-T signals more heavily for Arabic YMYL content"
  3. "Schedule your Arabic content publication for Sunday-Thursday (MENA work week) for maximum engagement"

### Section 3: Customer Spotlight (100-150 words)
- Short case study or user story
- Format: who they are, what they achieved, one key metric
- Rotating between segments (publisher, agency, enterprise)
- Include customer photo/logo with permission
- Example: "How Al Arabiya generates 40 articles/week with ChainIQ"

### Section 4: Product Update (100-150 words)
- What shipped last month
- 3-5 bullet points of new features or improvements
- Screenshot of the most visual/impactful feature
- Link to full changelog
- Roadmap teaser: "Coming next month: [feature name]"

### Section 5: CTA Block
- **Trial users:** "Upgrade to unlock [feature] — [plan] starts at $500/mo"
- **Paying customers:** "Have you tried [new feature]? Here's how →"
- **Enterprise prospects:** "See how [customer] scaled to 100 articles/week — book a demo"
- **Leads:** "Start your free 14-day trial — no credit card required"

### Footer
- Social links: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube
- Unsubscribe link (required)
- Physical address (required by CAN-SPAM)
- "You're receiving this because you [signed up for ChainIQ / downloaded our report / etc.]"
- Preference center link: "Update your email preferences"

## Deliverability Setup

### DNS Records for chainiq.io

#### SPF Record
```
Type: TXT
Host: @
Value: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com include:[ESP-specific] ~all
```
*Replace [ESP-specific] with your email service provider's SPF include (e.g., include:spf.convertkit.com)*

#### DKIM Record
```
Type: CNAME
Host: [selector]._domainkey
Value: [provided by email service provider]
```
*Your ESP will provide the exact selector and value during setup*

#### DMARC Record
```
Type: TXT
Host: _dmarc
Value: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc-reports@chainiq.io; pct=100
```
*Start with p=none (monitoring only). After 30 days of clean reports, move to p=quarantine. After 60 days, move to p=reject.*

## Arabic Newsletter Considerations

### Bilingual Format
- Hero feature: English with Arabic summary paragraph
- Tips: Alternate between English and Arabic tips
- Customer spotlight: Language matches the customer
- Product update: English (technical terms are easier in English for most MENA professionals)

### RTL Handling
- Test RTL rendering in all major email clients
- Use direction: rtl CSS property for Arabic sections
- Ensure CTA buttons work in both LTR and RTL contexts
- Test on Android (common in MENA) and iOS

### Arabic Subject Lines
- A/B test Arabic vs. English subject lines per segment
- Arabic subject lines may drive higher opens in the UAE/KSA/Egypt segments
- Keep Arabic subjects under 40 characters (shorter display on mobile)
- Avoid mixing Arabic and English in the same subject line (rendering issues)
