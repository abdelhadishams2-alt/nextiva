## Review Generation Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta Users (Weeks 1-4)
- Ask all 10 beta users to leave reviews on G2 and Capterra
- Provide direct links to review pages
- Offer: "Your review helps other Arabic publishers find the right tools"
- Target: 5 reviews across platforms

### Phase 2: Early Customers (Weeks 4-8)
- Add review request to post-onboarding email (after 30 days of usage)
- In-app prompt: "Enjoying ChainIQ? Leave a review on G2" (after 10 articles generated)
- Personal email from founder requesting review
- Target: 5 additional reviews

### Phase 3: Ongoing (Month 3+)
- Automated review request in email after 60 days of active usage
- Quarterly review campaigns tied to product milestones
- Monitor and respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Target: 2-3 new reviews/month

### Review Response Guidelines
- **5-star reviews:** Thank them, highlight specific feature they mentioned
- **4-star reviews:** Thank them, ask what would make it 5 stars, log feedback
- **3-star or below:** Respond publicly acknowledging the issue, offer to resolve via email, fix the issue, follow up
- Response time: within 48 hours for all reviews
