# Tutorial: Your First Article in 5 Minutes

> **Difficulty:** Beginner | **Time:** 5 minutes | **Prerequisites:** ChainIQ account

---

## Step 1: Sign In

Navigate to your ChainIQ dashboard and sign in with your email and password.

## Step 2: Navigate to Generate

Click **Generate** in the sidebar navigation.

## Step 3: Enter Your Topic

Type your article topic in the topic field. For best results, use a specific keyword phrase:
- Good: "best project management tools for remote teams 2026"
- Too broad: "project management"

## Step 4: Configure Options (Optional)

- **Language:** Auto-detect (default) or select manually
- **Framework:** Auto-detect (default) — matches your project's tech stack
- **Content Length:** Standard (~2000 words)
- **Images:** Enabled (4-6 AI-generated images)

## Step 5: Generate

Click **Generate Article** and watch the real-time progress:
1. Project Analysis (10-20s)
2. Research — 6 rounds (60-120s)
3. Architecture (30-60s)
4. Image Generation (30-60s)
5. Draft Writing (60-120s)

## Step 6: Review

When complete, your article appears in the **Articles** tab. Review the content, structure, and images.

## Step 7: Edit (Optional)

Hover over any section and click **Edit** to refine it. Describe the change in plain language (e.g., "Add a comparison table for the top 3 tools").

## Step 8: Publish (Optional)

If you've connected a CMS (WordPress, Shopify, Ghost), click **Publish** to create a draft on your site.
