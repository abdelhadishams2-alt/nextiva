There's a false expectation that AI should be flawless out of the gate. That it can go from 0-60 in 0.001 seconds. The truth is, AI needs context. Just like teaching a friend a new skill, you start small, test, and adjust. You build that context over time. Mistakes will happen and they're part of how the system gets smarter.
